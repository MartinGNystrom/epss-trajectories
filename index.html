EPSS · Exploit Prediction Scoring System

Four lives of an EPSS score

Every vulnerability's EPSS score — the modeled probability of exploitation in the next 30 days — traces one of a few archetypal paths. Four real CVEs, one per archetype. Dashed rules mark EPSS model-version cutovers, where scores can step without any change in attacker behavior. Hover any chart for the exact score and percentile.

Reading the dashed rules: EPSS is periodically retrained — v3 (Mar 7, 2023), v4 (Mar 17, 2025), v5 (Jun 15, 2026). Each cutover rescores the whole population overnight: at the v5 boundary only 42% of CVEs scoring ≥0.9 kept that score. A score move across a dashed rule reflects the model changing its mind, not the threat changing its behavior — the honest signal is the shape between the rules.
